About Us

Welcome to Patrona – The Pet Enciclopedia, your trusted source for everything you need to know about your beloved pets.

At Patrona, we are passionate about animals and dedicated to providing clear, reliable, and easy-to-understand information about cats, dogs, and rabbits.

Whether you’re a new pet owner or an experienced animal lover, our goal is to help you better care for and understand your furry companions.

From health tips and nutrition guides to behavior insights and breed information, we cover a wide range of topics to support you in giving your pets a happy and healthy life.

We believe every pet deserves love, care, and knowledge—and Patrona is here to make that easier for you.

Thank you for visiting, and welcome to our pet-loving community!
